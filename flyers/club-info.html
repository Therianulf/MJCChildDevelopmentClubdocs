Do you want to understand, inspire, and advocate for children? Your journey to making an impact starts here.

🌍 Who We Are

We're a group of MJC students passionate about Child Development. Whether you're preparing for a career in the field or just want to explore the topic with a supportive community of peers, you are welcome here.

🌸 What We Do

  • Dive into new Child Development topics in a relaxed setting.
  • Network and build lasting friendships with fellow MJC students.
  • Attend MJC community events together.
  • Develop professional skills and prepare for your future career.
  • Host guest speakers and hands-on workshops led by specialists.
